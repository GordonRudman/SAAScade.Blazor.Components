# Master Individual Contributor Assignment Agreement

This Master Individual Contributor Assignment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between **SAAScade LTD**, a corporation ("Company") in The Republic of Ireland, and the individual accepting this Agreement ("Contributor").

Effective as of the date of electronic acceptance ("Effective Date").

---

## 1. Scope and Application

This Agreement governs all Contributions submitted by Contributor to Company, whether submitted before, on, or after the Effective Date.

This Agreement applies to all repositories, projects, products, services, and materials owned or controlled by Company, whether now existing or created in the future.

This is a one-time agreement and applies to all present and future Contributions.

---

## 2. Definitions

**"Contribution"** means any source code, object code, documentation, designs, inventions, discoveries, improvements, ideas, feedback, data, specifications, or other materials submitted or provided by Contributor to Company by any means.

---

## 3. Assignment of Intellectual Property

Contributor irrevocably assigns to Company all worldwide right, title, and interest in and to each Contribution, including:

- All copyrights  
- All rights to register and enforce copyrights  
- All rights to sue for past, present, and future infringement  
- All derivative works rights  
- All associated goodwill  

To the extent permitted by law, Contributions shall be deemed works made for hire. If not, they are assigned under this Section.

Contributor agrees to execute documents reasonably necessary to perfect or enforce Company’s rights.

---

## 4. Patent Assignment

Contributor irrevocably assigns to Company all right, title, and interest in and to any inventions, discoveries, improvements, and patent rights embodied in or related to the Contributions, including all patent applications and patents worldwide.

If assignment is not permitted by law, Contributor grants Company a perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free, transferable, sublicensable license under such patent rights.

---

## 5. Moral Rights Waiver

To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, Contributor waives and agrees not to assert any moral rights in the Contributions.

---

## 6. Representations and Warranties

Contributor represents and warrants that:

- Contributor has full authority to enter into this Agreement.
- Contributions are original or Contributor has full authority to assign them.
- Contributions do not infringe, misappropriate, or violate any third-party rights.
- No Contribution is subject to licenses requiring Company to disclose proprietary source code.
- If Contributor is employed, Contributor has obtained all necessary employer permissions.

---

## 7. Indemnification

Contributor shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Company and its officers, directors, employees, successors, and assigns from and against any and all claims, damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys’ fees) arising out of or relating to:

- Any breach of Contributor’s representations or warranties;
- Any claim that a Contribution infringes or misappropriates third-party intellectual property rights;
- Any failure to obtain required employer or third-party permissions.

This obligation survives termination of this Agreement.

---

## 8. License Back

Company grants Contributor a perpetual, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use their individual Contributions for personal and professional portfolio purposes only.

---

## 9. Confidentiality

Contributor shall not disclose non-public information relating to the Project without prior written consent.

---

## 10. Binding Arbitration

Any dispute, claim, or controversy arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be resolved exclusively by binding arbitration administered by the American Arbitration Association (AAA) under its Commercial Arbitration Rules.

- The arbitration shall take place in The Republic of Ireland.
- The arbitration shall be conducted in English.
- The arbitrator’s decision shall be final and binding.
- Judgment on the award may be entered in any court of competent jurisdiction.

Nothing in this section prevents Company from seeking injunctive relief in a court of competent jurisdiction for intellectual property violations.

---

## 11. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of The Republic of Ireland, without regard to conflict-of-law principles.

---

## 12. Survival

Sections relating to intellectual property ownership, patent rights, moral rights waiver, indemnification, arbitration, and governing law shall survive termination.

---

## 13. Electronic Acceptance

Electronic acceptance constitutes a legally binding signature and applies to Contributions made using Contributor’s authenticated account.
